More Information

The educational system in STEM is different from regular high schools because they have something called a capstone project each semester. The goal of the project every semester is to present solutions for at least two of Egypt's Grand challenges, with fixed restrictions such as a prototype cost not exceeding 1000 pounds and a team size of no more than 5 members. Additionally, the project must fulfill at least two design requirements apart from the cost. The full capstone project includes not only a prototype but also a Poster, which is a summary of the project, a Portfolio, consisting of a personal introduction and five learning experiences that helped you learn throughout the project, and a presentation, which is a 20-minute presentation that tells the story of the project and the problem we tried to solve. To achieve this, I had to acquire several skills like leadership, critical thinking, problem-solving, academic writing, working under pressure, and teamwork.
